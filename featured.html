Sri Yantra

The Sri Yantra is a mystical diagram. It consists of nine interlocking triangles - four upward ones which represent Shiva, and five downward ones representing Shakti. In my representation you can see the mixing of varnas in the middle two triangles and the separation of heaven (dipicted in blue) and earth (dipicted in red).

Sketch from a book

This shape is not a painting, it is a digital drawing. This was my first attempt to fully add color to a sketch or drawing. Note on my art: I only use primary and secondary colors and the occasional indigo. I also like to apply the complementary color theory to my work as you will see in the next painting.

Five pentagrams in a pentagon

In this painting I colored the pentagrams to appear as stars. The Spirit (depicted by the color yellow) and the people (depicted by the color purple) are central and on top, and earth (red) is above heaven (blue).
