A well known geometric symbol in the world of Sacred Geometry symbolizing creation. It’s a geometric pattern of repeating, overlapping circles of an equal radius. It is  believed to hold the most meaningful and sacred patterns of the universe.

Vector Equilibrium

Equilibrium of vectors simply means that the sum of the vectors equals zero. It is the only geometric symbol where all of the vectors are of equal length. It represents the ultimate and perfect condition wherein the movement of energy comes to a state of absolute equilibrium, and therefore absolute stillness and nothingness.

Merkaba

The word Merkaba denotes the notion of a chariot in Hebrew and appears in the Bible when prophets ascend towards the heavens in chariots of fire. The symbol is made of 2 intersecting tetrahedrons that spin in opposite directions. It combines opposing forces in perfect balance. This union in balance activates the light that can transport us to higher dimensions.
