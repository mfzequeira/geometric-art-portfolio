Vesica Pisis

In sacred geometry, creation began with a central point referred to as The Spirit or God and a surrounding circle. When the spirit moved to the edge of the circle it created another circle. This is the shape it makes. It’s called vesica piscis because the space where the two circles overlap looks like the bladder of a fish.

Egg of Life

The egg of life is the second iteration of creation in sacred geometry. It has been used to represent new life, health, and fertility. It embodies the idea of rebirth and rejuvenation in the cycle of life. I like to think that the blue and the red parts of the vesica piscis from the previous image must have divided by three. This shape can be infinitely large and small also, giving a new meaning to the cycle of life

Fruit of Life

The fruit of life is the holiest shape in sacred geometry because it is hidden behind the flower of life. It’s the third iteration in creation and in this series it looks like the six spheres around the center one multiplied and arranged themselves in lines moving outward.
